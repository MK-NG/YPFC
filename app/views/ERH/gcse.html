A Levels

This subject based qualification, for those aged 16 and over, can lead to university, further study, training, or work.

{# APPRENTICESHIP CARD #}

Apprenticeship

For those aged 16 and over, combine working with studying to gain skills and experience in a specific job.

{# SUPPORTED INTERNSHIP CARD #}

Supported internship

For those aged 16 to 24, these internships provide extra support for those with an EHC to move into employment.

{# T LEVELS CARD #}

T Levels

A 2-year qualification that includes a 45-day industry placement for students aged 16 to 19.

{# TRAINEESHIP CARD #}

Traineeship

A course for 16 to 24-year-olds that includes work experience to help you get ready for work or an apprenticeship.

Work-related qualifications for 16 to 19-year-olds available in a wide range of career areas.
