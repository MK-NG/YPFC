Kickstart job

Ready for the world of work but don’t know where to start? With a Kickstart job you can gain 6 months work experience whilst being paid. If you’re aged 16 to 24 and claiming universal credit, this scheme offers you the opportunity to gain the skills employers need to help you find your next job.

This scheme offers you the opportunity to gain the skills employers need to help you find your next job.

How to apply for a Kickstart job

1. Talk to a Work coach at JobCentre Plus

Speak to a Work coach at your nearest Job Centre Plus branch to discuss your skills and interests.

2. Get matched to a job vacancy

The Work coach will use this information to help match you to any suitable Kickstart job vacancies and give advice on the roles.

3. Put in your application

You can then apply for these job placements online. Speak to a Work coach for advice about making an application and what to include.

4. Get interview ready

You may then be invited for an interview with the employer who is running the Kickstart job placement. You should prepare for the interview by thinking about what you want to gain from the job placement and what skills you can offer as an employee (for more advice on interviews visit the National Careers Service (opens in a new tab).

5. Start your new job

If you’re successful at the interview stage the employer will offer you the job placement and contact you to arrange a start date.

Head to Job Centre Plus to find your Kickstart job placement.
