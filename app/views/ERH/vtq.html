Vocational technical qualifications (VTQs)

Vocational technical qualifications (also known as VTQs) are practical qualifications that relate to a specific job or career sector.

Aimed at those 16 and over, VTQs are designed to help you to get the skills needed to start a job, progress in a career, or go on to higher levels of education.

VTQs can usually be taken at a college.

{# who #}

Who can apply for VTQs

To start VTQs, you’ll need to be:

  • 16 or over
  • living in England
  • not in full-time education

You can apply for VTQs while you’re still at school but you’ll need to be 16 or over by the end of the summer holidays to start the VTQ.

{# include #}

What do you need take a VTQ

You normally need:

  • at least 5 GCSEs at grades 3 to 1
  • a level 1 qualification
