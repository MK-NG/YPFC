Understanding your options

Whether you got the grades you were expecting or not, there are various education options after your GCSEs. You might choose to resit your assessments, continue your studies or combine work and studying.

If you need to rethink your next steps, you still have lots of different options to choose from. You can speak to your school or college to understand all the routes you could take, or visit GOV.UK to appeal against an exam result.

If you need help or advice around your exam results and next steps, you can call us on 0800 100 900 or use webchat.
