Start a business

If you are 18 or over and have a great business idea, starting your own business is an option.

Where do I start?

The Prince’s Trust has a wide range of resources to help you get started, including business plan templates and finance advice.

There are also government-backed schemes that can provide help and support for business start-ups.

Can I get funding

If you’re aged 18 to 30, you can apply for the Prince’s Trust Enterprise programme which offers funding from £1000 to £5000.

How do I set up my business

What you need to do will depend on:

  • the type of business
  • where you work
  • if you have employees

Check to see what you need to do to set up your business.
