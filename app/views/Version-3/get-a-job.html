Get a job

A job allows you to earn money and gain experience in a chosen field and can be full-time or part-time.

If you want to know more about how to get a job, there is advice available that can help you land the role that’s right for you.

Which job is right for me

You can explore job profiles to find out what a specific role involves and if it’s the right fit for you.

Where do I find job vacancies

You can explore the different ways of looking for a new job to ensure you don’t miss any suitable job advertisements.

Writing a CV and cover letter

A CV is a short, written summary of your skills, achievements and experience.

You should introduce yourself to an employer using a cover letter alongside your CV.

The National Careers Service offers advice on how to write a CV and cover letter.

If I get an interview

If your application is successful you may be invited to interview.

If you would like advice on how to prepare for an interview, here are some interview tips.
