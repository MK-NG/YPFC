    Combine work and study

    Combine work and study to gain skills and experience in a specific job. Explore the available options below:

    {# Vocational Technical Qualifications (VTQs) CARD #}

    Vocational Technical Qualifications (VTQs)

    A VTQ gives the opportunity for those aged 16 to 19 years old to gain skills and experience with a work-based qualification in a wide range of career options.

    {# # T LEVELS CARD #}

    T Levels

    T-levels give 16 to 19-year-olds the opportunity to get real work experience with an industry placement whilst gaining a qualification that employers look for.

    {# APPRENTICESHIP CARD #}

    Apprenticeship

    Apprenticeships are industry-recognised qualifications for those aged 16 and over and provide the opportunity to get hands-on experience by working whilst studying.

    {# TRAINEESHIP CARD #}

    Traineeship

    Traineeships give 16 to 24-year-olds the opportunity to gain the skills and work experience needed to move into an apprenticeship.

    {# SCHOOL LEAVERS SCHEMES #}

    School Leavers Schemes

    Aimed at those who have completed A levels, this scheme allows you to work at a large company and earn money whilst you train.

    Continue your studies

    Open up a range of opportunities for your future by going into further education. Explore the available courses below:

    {# HIGHER EDUCATION CARD #}

    Higher education

    For those aged 18 and over, this classroom-based technical training qualification can give you a step up in your chosen career or lead to further study.

    {# A-LEVELS CARD #}

    A Levels

    A levels are subject-based qualifications for those aged 16 and over and could lead to university, further study, or starting work.

    {# 1 YEAR FUNDED COURSE CARD #} {#

    1 year funded course

    A free course for those aged 18 to 19 to gain work-related or technical skills needed by employers.

    Get straight into work

    Are you ready to get into the world of work but not sure where to start? Explore the available work schemes below:

    {# HTQs) CARD #}

    Higher technical skills courses (HTQs)

    For those aged 18 and over, this classroom-based technical training qualification can give you a step up in your chosen career or lead to further study.

    {# KICKSTART CARD #}

    Kickstart job

    For 16 to 24-year-olds claiming universal credit, this 6-month work experience scheme offers the opportunity to gain the skills employers need.

    {# SUPPORTED INTERNSHIP CARD #}

    Supported internship

    For those aged 16 to 24 with an education, health and care plan (EHC), a supported internship provides the support needed to make the move into employment.

    {# INTERNSHIPS AND VOLUNTEERING CARD #}

    Internships and volunteering

    For those aged 16 and over, gain work experience in a chosen job and learn about the industry through an internship or by volunteering.

    {# GET A JOB #}
