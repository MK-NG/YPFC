School leavers scheme

Aimed at those who have completed A levels, work at a large company and earn money whilst you train.

The school leavers scheme is mainly aimed at those who have completed A levels. It allows you to learn and train with a large company while earning a wage.

Starting a school leavers scheme

To start a school leavers scheme, you’ll need to:

  • be 18 or over
  • not be in full-time education
  • have a keen interest in the sector you want to work in

Usually, the schemes require high A level grades or equivalent qualifications.

You can apply for a school leavers scheme while you’re still at school, but you’ll need to be 18 or over by the end of the summer holidays to start it.

What you’ll do on the school leavers scheme

You’ll gain the skills and experience that employers look for through learning while working and earning a wage.

The scheme may also include:

  • rotating between different locations with the same company
  • distance learning or time in college or university

Sectors you could work in

Sectors included in the scheme are:

  • accountancy
  • engineering
  • finance
  • law
  • leisure
  • retail

Applying for a school leavers scheme

You can find out if a company offers a school leavers scheme on their website, alongside information on how to apply.
